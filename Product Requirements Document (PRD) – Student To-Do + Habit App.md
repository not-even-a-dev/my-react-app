**Product Requirements Document (PRD) – Student To-Do \+ Habit App**

---

### **1\. Overview / Purpose**

This project is a **web-based to-do and habit tracking application** designed primarily for **students** who want to manage their academic and personal responsibilities in a fun and motivating way. The app should feel lighthearted, visually engaging, and rewarding—transforming productivity into an enjoyable daily habit rather than a chore.

---

### **2\. Target Users**

**Primary Audience:** Students (high school, college, or university).

**Secondary Audience:** Anyone who enjoys gamified productivity systems or habit trackers.

**User Persona Example:**

* **Name:** Sofia

* **Age:** 20

* **Occupation:** College Student

* **Goals:** Stay on top of coursework, form productive study habits, and manage extracurricular tasks.

* **Pain Points:** Overwhelmed by multiple apps, unmotivated by plain to-do lists, needs a clear yet fun way to track progress.

---

### **3\. Key Objectives**

* Create a **fun, visually dynamic**, and **gamified** task management experience.

* Combine **habit tracking** and **task management** into a single interface.

* Prioritize **usability, clarity, and motivation** over complexity.

* Provide actionable feedback and small rewards to encourage daily engagement.

---

### **4\. Platform**

* **Platform:** Web-only (optimized for desktop and mobile browsers)

---

### **5\. Core Features (All Included in Version 1\)**

#### **Task Management**

* **Task Creation** with title, description, due date, and optional tags/projects.

* **Due Dates & Reminders** via email or in-browser notifications.

* **Recurring Tasks** (e.g., daily study routine, weekly review).

* **Subtasks** for breaking down larger assignments.

* **Drag-and-Drop Reordering** to prioritize visually.

* **Tags/Projects** to organize by subjects, courses, or personal projects.

* **Clean, Minimal UI** designed with students in mind—no clutter.

#### **Habit Tracking**

* **Create Habits** (e.g., "Drink water", "Study 30 min", "Read a chapter").

* **Track Streaks** and visualize progress with playful animations or icons.

* **Gamified Feedback:** Award points or badges for task completions and habit streaks.

* **Progress Dashboard:** Show daily/weekly overview of accomplishments.

#### **Notifications**

* Smart **in-browser notifications** for upcoming due dates or habit reminders.

* Optional **push notifications** (browser-based) for key milestones or streak alerts.

---

### **6\. Design & User Experience**

* **Theme:** Playful, colorful, yet minimal and easy to navigate.

* **Visual Style:** Rounded edges, pastel tones, dynamic micro-animations (confetti, progress bars, streak flames).

* **Tone:** Encouraging and friendly—celebrate progress rather than penalize missed tasks.

* **Usability Goals:**

  * Clear, one-click access to creating and editing tasks.

  * Smooth drag-and-drop and transitions.

  * Mobile-responsive layout without losing visual clarity.

---

### **7\. Out of Scope (for Now)**

* Collaboration or shared task lists.

* Advanced analytics or reporting.

* Mobile app or offline mode.

---

### **8\. Version 1 Summary**

Version 1 (MVP) will include all listed features with a polished, gamified interface and habit-tracking integration. Future updates may expand into collaborative features, themes, and deeper gamification mechanics.
